Welcome

Our mission is to educate and inspire the community to conserve water and reduce waste through simple daily actions. Every effort counts — together, we can make a cleaner, greener environment!

About This Project

This website was created to raise awareness about the importance of water conservation and proper waste management. It serves as an educational hub for students, families, and local communities to learn how to protect natural resources.

Why It Matters

Water Conservation

Did you know that millions of people around the world still lack access to clean water? Conserving water helps ensure future generations can enjoy this essential resource.

Waste Management

Proper waste segregation prevents pollution and keeps our oceans and rivers clean. Reducing plastic use makes a huge difference!

Eco-Friendly Tips

  • Turn off the faucet while brushing your teeth.
  • Reuse water for plants or cleaning when possible.
  • Bring reusable bags, bottles, and containers.
  • Segregate biodegradable and non-biodegradable waste.
  • Join local cleanup drives or start one in your neighborhood!

Did You Know?

Over 80% of wastewater flows back into the ecosystem without proper treatment. Small actions at home can make a global difference.
